Website Evaluations

Dribbble.com

Dribbble is a platform for designers to showcase their work. It has a clean and professional design.

  • Contrast: The site effectively uses contrast to highlight important elements.
  • Repetition: Consistent design patterns are used throughout the site, enhancing usability.
  • Alignment: Elements are precisely aligned, creating a polished look.
  • Proximity: Related elements are grouped logically, improving information flow.

Lingscars.com

Lingscars.com is known for its chaotic and overwhelming design. It's an example of what not to do.

  • Contrast: The site uses excessive and clashing colors, hindering readability.
  • Repetition: Random elements are repeated without a clear purpose, creating visual noise.
  • Alignment: Elements are randomly scattered, lacking any sense of organization.
  • Proximity: Related elements are not grouped logically, resulting in a confusing layout.
