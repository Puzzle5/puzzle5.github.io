Meet Bartholomew

Bartholomew is the heart and soul of Earthbloom Minerals. With his keen sense of smell and cheerful thumbs-up, he's always ready to find the next sparkly treasure. He's not just a mole; he's a symbol of our commitment to gentle, joyful mining.

He enjoys long walks in the tunnels, sniffing out rare minerals, and the occasional earthworm snack. His infectious enthusiasm brings a smile to everyone's face.

"Every sparkle tells a story." - Bartholomew
Deep within the earth's crust, where glittering geodes whisper secrets to the patient stone, lies Proud Cobra Minerals, inc., a company less concerned with brute extraction and more with coaxing the earth's treasures into the light. Picture a team of geologists who carry magnifying glasses and teacups, pausing their seismic readings for a spot of Earl Grey. Their mining equipment isn't just drills and dynamite; it's a collection of enchanted trowels, crystal-tuned sonic hammers, and gentle, earthworm-powered conveyor belts. Instead of blasting, they prefer to "serenade" the minerals, using harmonious frequencies to loosen the gemstones and ores from their rocky slumber. Their process involves a bit of geological gardening, carefully tending to the mineral veins like prize-winning petunias. Each extracted crystal is polished with moonbeams and whispered affirmations, ensuring it carries only the purest, most cheerful vibrations. Their company mascot? A fluffy mole named "Bartholomew," who has an uncanny knack for sniffing out the most sparkly finds. Earthbloom Minerals doesn't just mine; they perform a delicate dance with the earth, leaving behind not scars, but blooming crystal gardens and the gentle hum of happy geology.
